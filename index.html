Appointment

Schedule an appointment.

Book one-on-one tutoring time with the HALC in a couple of minutes — pick a course, pick a time, and you're set.

Schedule Your Appointment ↗
01

Click "Schedule Your Appointment."

It opens the HALC's official booking form in a new tab — nothing to download first.

02

Sign in with your CUNYfirst username.

Use your CUNYfirst login (e.g. John.Doe72@login.cuny.edu) — the same one you use for CUNY apps.

03

Log in again on the CUNY portal.

After Microsoft, you'll hit a second CUNY login screen — enter the same username and password, then click Login.

04

Click Start Now and answer the questions.

This kicks off the Tutoring Request Form.

05

Choose your modality and subject.

Pick in-person or virtual, then select the subject you need help with.

06

Pick a tutor and time from the table.

The schedule shows which tutor is available for each day and hour.

07

Select a date and time, add your phone number.

Fill in your appointment date, time, and phone number, then hit Submit.

08

Watch your email.

You'll get a confirmation with your tutor, time, and room (or a Teams link if it's virtual).
