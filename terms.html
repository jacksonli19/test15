Tutoring Policy

Students are expected to arrive on time for their tutoring sessions and must adhere to the university's academic integrity policies throughout. If a student is unable to attend a scheduled session, they should notify the tutoring center at halctutoring@hostos.cuny.edu.

Students should come prepared with relevant course materials, textbooks, and assignments, and bring specific questions to each session. Tutors are here to help students develop a deeper understanding of course content, prepare for exams, and strengthen problem-solving skills. To get the most out of tutoring, students are encouraged to attend regularly rather than relying solely on occasional sessions.
